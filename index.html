Digital Magazine Team Structure

Branches, roles, and responsibilities

Core Leadership & Management

Project Lead / Managing Editor

  • Owns overall strategy and timeline.
  • Meets board members and active group.
  • Gives final publication approval.

Editorial & Content Branch

Writers, editors, translators, proofreaders

  • Editors refine submissions with writers.
  • 10 to 20 contributors submit one piece each.
  • Proofreaders and professors perform final checks.

Creative & Design Branch

Cover direction and interior design

  • Art Director defines Minimalist, Vintage, or Bold style.
  • Cover Designer creates high-impact launch cover.
  • Interior Designers format pieces for flipbook reading.
  • Visual Coordinator manages photography and stock sourcing.

Distribution & Digital Specialist

Publishing and outreach delivery

  • Digital Publisher prepares and uploads the flipbook.
  • Marketing Coordinator runs launch promotion.
  • Performance tracking uses analytics and social channels.

Operational Workflow

Five stages from submissions to launch

Step 01

Submission Phase

Send work submission forms and hire the design team.

Step 02

Curation Phase

Editors and proofreaders select the best 10 to 20 works.

Step 03

Design Phase

Designers style assigned works based on the chosen aesthetic.

Step 04

Final Review

Project Lead and advisor review the fully formatted magazine.

Step 05

Digital Launch

Host on a flipbook platform and share with the community.
